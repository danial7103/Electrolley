How to use Electrolley

1. Scan the items you wanna buy to the barcode scanner

2. When the item has display at the table press add to cart button

3. Click the shopping bag icon to display cart

4. Customer can change quantity of item or remove the whole item

5. Customer may proceed to payment by click the checkout button

6. Choice of payment such as Stripe and NFC card

Note: no cash payment supported
